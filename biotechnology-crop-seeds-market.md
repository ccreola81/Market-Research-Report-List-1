<p><p>The biotechnology crop seeds market includes Herbicide Tolerant (HT) and Insect Tolerant (IT) varieties. HT seeds are genetically modified to withstand specific herbicides, allowing farmers to control weeds without harming crops, improving yield and efficiency. IT seeds are engineered to produce proteins toxic to certain pests, reducing the need for chemical insecticides and minimizing crop damage. Both types enhance agricultural productivity, reduce input costs, and promote sustainable farming practices, addressing challenges like pest resistance and weed management in modern agriculture.</p></p>

<p><p>The Anti Submarine Rocket Launching System (ASRLS) is designed for targeting submarines using specialized rockets. The market features three primary types: Hot Launch, where rockets are ignited within the launch tube; Cold Launch, where rockets are ejected before ignition, enhancing safety; and Concentric Canister Launch, where multiple rockets are housed in a compact system, allowing for rapid deployment. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of safety, efficiency, and operational flexibility, catering to diverse naval requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>High Density PTFE Membranes are categorized into three main types based on their construction and reinforcement. </p><p>1. **PE Membranes**: These are typically embedded with polyethylene, offering enhanced resistance and flexibility for various applications.</p><p>2. **Non-Reinforced Membranes**: These are pure PTFE products that provide excellent chemical resistance and are lightweight, ideal for specific filtration needs.</p><p>3. **Titanium Reinforced Membranes**: These combine PTFE with titanium fibers, providing increased strength and durability, making them suitable for demanding environments and applications requiring high performance.</p></p>

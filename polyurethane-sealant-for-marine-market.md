<p><strong>The Polyurethane Sealant for Marine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane sealants in the marine market are categorized into single-component and multi-component types. Single-component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure through moisture absorption, offering ease of application and long-lasting flexibility. They are ideal for general sealing and bonding tasks in marine environments. Conversely, multi-component sealants consist of two or more components that must be mixed prior to application. This type typically provides superior performance, durability, and chemical resistance, making it suitable for demanding marine applications like hull sealing and structural bonding.</p></p>

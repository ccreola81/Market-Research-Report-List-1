<p><p>A diaphragm sealed manometer is a precision measurement instrument designed to gauge pressure by utilizing a flexible diaphragm that isolates the measuring fluid from the pressure source. <p><ul><li>U Tube Manometers</li><li>Inclined Tube Manometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diaphragm sealed manometer market is characterized by two main types: U-tube manometers and inclined tube manometers. U-tube manometers consist of a U-shaped tube filled with liquid, measuring pressure differences between two points through the liquid's height. Inclined tube manometers, on the other hand, use a slanted tube to provide enhanced sensitivity and accuracy for small pressure changes by increasing the length measurement scale. Both types are essential in various industries for accurate pressure monitoring and control.</p></p>

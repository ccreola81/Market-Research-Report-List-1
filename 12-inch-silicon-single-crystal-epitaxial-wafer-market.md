<p><p>The 12-Inch Silicon Single Crystal Epitaxial Wafer market is categorized into two primary types: Single Layer Epitaxy and Multilayer Epitaxy. Single Layer Epitaxy involves depositing a single layer of silicon onto a substrate, ensuring high purity and uniformity, ideal for various semiconductor applications. In contrast, Multilayer Epitaxy allows for the stacking of multiple silicon layers, enabling the fabrication of complex structures with enhanced electrical properties. Both types cater to different technological needs, influencing applications in electronics and optoelectronics.</p></p>

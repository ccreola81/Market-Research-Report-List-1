<p><ul><li>Composite Material</li><li>dental Porcelain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veneer dentistry primarily utilizes two types of materials: composite and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made from a tooth-colored resin, offering a more cost-effective and less invasive solution, easily customizable in color and shape. In contrast, dental porcelain veneers are crafted from durable, high-quality ceramics, providing superior aesthetics and stain resistance. While composite veneers can usually be applied in a single visit, porcelain veneers often require multiple appointments due to the meticulous fabrication process, enhancing resilience and natural appearance.</p></p>

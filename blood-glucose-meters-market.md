<p><ul><li>Electrode Type Blood Glucose Meter</li><li>Photoelectric Blood Glucose Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood glucose meter market comprises various types, primarily categorized as electrode-type and photoelectric blood glucose meters. Electrode-type meters utilize biosensors that detect glucose levels through electrochemical reactions, offering quick and accurate readings with minimal blood samples. In contrast, photoelectric blood glucose meters employ light-based technologies, measuring glucose concentration by analyzing how light interacts with the blood. Both types cater to the needs of diabetic patients, providing essential tools for managing blood sugar levels effectively.</p></p>

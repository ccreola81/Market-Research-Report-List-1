<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) rubber is produced predominantly through two methods: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. </p><p>In solution polymerization, the monomers are dissolved in a solvent, allowing for better control over molecular weight and polymer structure, resulting in high-performance materials suitable for demanding applications. </p><p>Suspension polymerization involves dispersing monomers in water, leading to larger particle sizes and a less controlled molecular structure. This method is simpler and cost-effective, making it suitable for general-purpose applications in various industries.</p></p>

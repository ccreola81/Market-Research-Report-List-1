<p><strong>The All-Solid-State Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-Based All-Solid-State Battery</li><li>All-Solid-State Battery with Inorganic Solid Electrolytes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The All-Solid-State Battery market comprises two main types: polymer-based and those utilizing inorganic solid electrolytes. Polymer-based all-solid-state batteries utilize flexible and lightweight polymers as electrolytes, enhancing safety and energy density while offering easier manufacturing. In contrast, all-solid-state batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes leverage materials like ceramics, notoriously providing high ionic conductivity and thermal stability, which translates to improved performance and longevity. Both types aim to overcome limitations of traditional lithium-ion batteries, revolutionizing energy storage solutions.</p></p>

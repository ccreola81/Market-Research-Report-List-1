<p><p>The car sensors market includes various types essential for vehicle performance and emissions control. The **Throttle Position Sensor (TPS)** monitors throttle valve position, aiding fuel injection and engine management. The **Manifold Absolute Pressure (MAP) Sensor** measures intake manifold pressure, optimizing air-fuel mixture. The **Mass Airflow Sensor (MAF)** calculates air entering the engine, ensuring efficient combustion. The **O2 Sensor (Oxygen Sensor)** detects exhaust oxygen levels, regulating emissions. Other sensors, like temperature and speed sensors, enhance safety and performance. Together, these sensors ensure optimal engine operation, fuel efficiency, and compliance with environmental standards.</p></p>

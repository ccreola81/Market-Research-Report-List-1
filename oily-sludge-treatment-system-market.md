<p><p>The Oily Sludge Treatment System market comprises primarily two types: Dehydration and Centrifugal. The Dehydration type utilizes thermal or mechanical processes to separate water from the sludge, effectively reducing its volume and facilitating further treatment. In contrast, the Centrifugal type employs high-speed rotation in a centrifuge to achieve separation of oil, water, and solids based on density differences. Both methods are essential for managing oily waste, improving recovery rates, and minimizing environmental impact in various industrial applications.</p></p>

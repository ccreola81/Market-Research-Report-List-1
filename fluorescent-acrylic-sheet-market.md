<p><strong>The Fluorescent Acrylic Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescent acrylic sheets are available in two primary types: cast acrylic sheets and extruded acrylic sheets. Cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring molten acrylic into a mold, resulting in a thicker, more durable product with superior optical clarity and color consistency. In contrast, extruded acrylic sheets are created by forcing acrylic through a die, making them thinner, more flexible, and generally more cost-effective. Both types are used in various applications, including signage, displays, and lighting due to their vibrant colors and light transmission properties.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Inline AOI Equipment</li><li>Offline AOI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) market is divided into two main types: inline and offline AOI equipment. Inline AOI equipment is integrated into the production line, enabling real-time inspection of printed circuit boards (PCBs) and reducing the risk of defects during manufacturing. Offline AOI equipment, on the other hand, operates independently, allowing for batch inspections after production. This type offers flexibility and can be used for thorough quality checks, ensuring high standards in PCB assembly and other electronic manufacturing processes.</p></p>

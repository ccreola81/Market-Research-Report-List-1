<p><p>The flexible adhesives market comprises three primary types: water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup, making them ideal for various applications. Solvent-based adhesives have higher performance characteristics and are suitable for bonding difficult surfaces, though they may pose environmental concerns. Other types include hot melt, pressure-sensitive adhesives, and reactive adhesives, each tailored for specific applications in industries like construction, automotive, and packaging, addressing diverse bonding needs.</p></p>

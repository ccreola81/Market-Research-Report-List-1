<p><p>Pulsed Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) is a powerful analytical technique used to measure the magnetic properties of atomic nuclei. Unlike continuous NMR, pulsed NMR uses short bursts of radiofrequency energy to acquire data, allowing for the observation of molecular dynamics and structure in various states. This technique is widely employed in fields such as chemistry, biology, and materials science for applications including substance identification, molecular characterization, and understanding complex molecular systems.</p><p>The Pulsed NMR Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period, driven by increased demand for advanced analytical techniques in research and industry. Key trends include the integration of NMR with other analytical methods, such as mass spectrometry and chromatography, to enhance data accuracy and efficiency. Furthermore, advancements in technology, like the miniaturization of NMR systems and the development of portable devices, are making this technique more accessible for routine analysis. The growing interest in pharmaceutical research and the demand for non-destructive testing in various sectors are also contributing to market growth.

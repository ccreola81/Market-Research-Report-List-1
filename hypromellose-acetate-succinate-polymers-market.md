<p><p>Hypromellose Acetate Succinate (HPMCAS) polymers are categorized into L Grade, M Grade, and H Grade, based on their viscosity and solubility characteristics. L Grade is low viscosity, suitable for immediate-release formulations. M Grade offers medium viscosity and is versatile for various formulations, while H Grade features high viscosity, ideal for sustained-release applications. Each grade meets specific pharmaceutical needs, influencing drug release profiles and enhancing formulation stability, thereby playing a crucial role in the development of oral dosage forms.</p></p>

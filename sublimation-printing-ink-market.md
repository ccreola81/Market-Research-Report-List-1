<p><p>Sublimation printing ink is categorized based on the type of fabric it is suited for. For polyester, the ink is specifically designed to bond with synthetic fibers, resulting in vibrant and durable prints. For polyester blends, the ink accommodates fabrics that combine polyester with other fibers, ensuring quality aesthetics and longevity. For cotton, special sublimation inks or pre-treated fabrics are required, as cotton does not naturally accept sublimation dyes, resulting in a different approach to achieve vivid prints.</p></p>

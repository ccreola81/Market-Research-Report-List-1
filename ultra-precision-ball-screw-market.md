<p><strong>The Ultra Precision Ball Screw Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ballscrew</li><li>External Circulation Ballscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra precision ball screw market is categorized into two main types: internal circulation and external circulation ball screws. Internal circulation ball screws feature balls that recirculate within a closed loop inside the screw, offering compact design and minimal footprint. External circulation ball screws, on the other hand, allow the balls to circulate outside the nut, providing easier replacement and reduced friction, thus facilitating higher speeds and load capacities. Both types cater to precision applications in various industries.</p></p>

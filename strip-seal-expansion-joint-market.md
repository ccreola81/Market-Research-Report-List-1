<p><p>Strip seal expansion joints are essential for accommodating thermal expansion and contraction in bridge structures and roadways. The market features two main types: strip seal expansion joints with straight heads, designed for straight applications where minimal angular movement is needed, providing a streamlined, efficient solution. Both types ensure effective sealing and protection against water and debris ingress.</p></p>

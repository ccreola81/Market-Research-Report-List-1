<p><p>The turbine oils market comprises two primary types: mineral oil-based turbine oils and synthetic oil-based turbine oils. Mineral oil-based turbine oils are derived from refined petroleum, offering effective lubrication and good thermal stability, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, synthetic oil-based turbine oils are engineered from chemical compounds, providing superior thermal stability, reduced volatility, and enhanced lubrication properties. These characteristics make synthetic oils preferable for high-performance and extreme conditions, driving their growing adoption in the turbine oil market.</p></p>

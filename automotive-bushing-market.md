<p><p>The automotive bushing market includes rubber and polyurethane types, each offering distinct properties. Rubber bushings are widely used due to their excellent vibration damping, noise reduction, and cost-effectiveness. They are ideal for standard vehicles but may wear faster under extreme conditions. Polyurethane bushings provide enhanced durability, stiffness, and resistance to wear, making them suitable for high-performance or heavy-duty applications. While polyurethane offers better handling and longevity, it can transmit more road noise compared to rubber, balancing performance and comfort differently.</p></p>

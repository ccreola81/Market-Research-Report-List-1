<p><p>The geotextile tube market is segmented into woven and nonwoven types. Woven geotextile tubes are made from interlaced fibers, offering high strength and durability, ideal for applications like erosion control and sediment management. Nonwoven geotextile tubes, on the other hand, consist of randomly arranged fibers, providing excellent filtration and drainage properties, commonly used in wastewater treatment and coastal protection. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing soil stability and environmental protection in civil and marine engineering projects.</p></p>

<p><p>The Porcelain-Fused-to-Metal (PFM) crown market is categorized into three main types based on the metal used: Titanium, Base-Metal Alloy, and Noble Metal Alloy. Titanium crowns offer high biocompatibility and strength, making them suitable for various dental applications. Base-Metal Alloys, such as nickel-chromium, are cost-effective and provide good durability, ideal for posterior teeth. Noble Metal Alloys, comprising gold, platinum, and palladium, offer excellent aesthetics and superior resistance to corrosion, often used for anterior crowns where appearance and longevity are crucial.</p></p>

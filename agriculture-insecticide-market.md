<p><p>The agriculture insecticide market encompasses various product types, primarily categorized into systemic and contact insecticides. Systemic insecticides are absorbed by plants and circulate through their tissues, providing long-lasting protection by targeting pests that feed on them. In contrast, contact insecticides act on pests upon direct contact, delivering immediate effects but requiring thorough application to be effective. Both types play critical roles in pest management strategies, ensuring crop health and maximizing agricultural productivity while minimizing damage from insect infestations.</p></p>

<p><p>The fluoropolymer film market comprises various types, each with unique properties and applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) offers high chemical resistance and is commonly used in electrical insulation. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) combines thermal stability with optical clarity, suitable for wire coatings. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) is noted for its high-temperature resistance and versatility in industrial applications. PVF (Polyvinyl Fluoride) provides excellent weather resistance, while PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) is used in chemical processing. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) is known for its strong mechanical properties and transparency, serving in architectural applications. Other fluoropolymers include niche materials with specialized uses across various industries.</p></p>

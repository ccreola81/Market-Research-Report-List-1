<p><ul><li>Single Lamp Pump Chamber</li><li>Double Lamp Pump Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser pump chamber market comprises primarily two types: single lamp pump chambers and double lamp pump chambers. Single lamp pump chambers utilize a single light source to excite the gain medium, offering a compact and cost-effective solution for smaller applications. In contrast, double lamp pump chambers feature two lamps, enhancing pumping efficiency and output power, making them suitable for high-performance lasers in industrial or scientific applications. Both types serve distinct needs based on power requirements and application complexity.</p></p>

<p><p>The heat treatment oils market consists of various types categorized by their operating temperatures and applications. Cold oils are used for quenching and cooling at lower temperatures, providing rapid cooling rates. Semi-hot oils function at moderate temperatures, offering a balance between cooling speed and thermal stability. Hot oils are utilized for high-temperature applications, ensuring effective heat transfer and minimizing oxidation. Bright oils, often used for surface hardening, provide superior finishes and reduced distortion, enhancing the quality of treated materials.</p></p>

<p><p>Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) is a process that rapidly freezes food items individually to preserve their quality, texture, and flavor. The IQF market is primarily divided into two types: Mechanical IQF and Cryogenic IQF. Mechanical IQF utilizes refrigeration systems, employing air or contact freezing methods to lower temperatures quickly, while Cryogenic IQF employs liquid nitrogen or carbon dioxide for ultra-fast freezing. Both methods ensure minimal ice crystal formation, enhancing food preservation and extending shelf life in the food industry.</p></p>

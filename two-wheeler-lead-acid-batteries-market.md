<p><ul><li>VRLA Batteries</li><li>Flooded Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler lead-acid batteries market primarily comprises two types: VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and designed to be spill-proof, making them ideal for modern two-wheelers. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance, as they are vented and can leak. Both types serve essential roles in providing reliable power and performance for two-wheelers, catering to varying consumer preferences and requirements in terms of convenience, maintenance, and cost.</p></p>

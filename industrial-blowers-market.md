<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blowers</li><li>Positive Displacement Blowers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial blowers market is primarily categorized into two types: centrifugal blowers and positive displacement blowers. Centrifugal blowers utilize rotational energy to increase air velocity, making them ideal for high-volume applications requiring consistent airflow. In contrast, positive displacement blowers function by trapping a fixed volume of air and forcing it out, offering higher pressure capabilities and efficiency for specific applications. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, including manufacturing, wastewater treatment, and HVAC systems, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>

<p><p>Mining collectors are specialized chemicals used in mineral processing to enhance the separation of valuable minerals from ores. Xanthates are widely utilized for sulfide mineral flotation, promoting effective recovery. Fatty acids and fatty amines serve as collectors for non-sulfide minerals, aiding in the flotation process. Dithiophosphates are effective for various sulfide minerals, enhancing selectivity in flotation. Dithiocarbonates act similarly but offer different selectivity profiles. Other collectors include unique formulations tailored for specific mineral types or processing conditions, optimizing recovery and efficiency.</p></p>

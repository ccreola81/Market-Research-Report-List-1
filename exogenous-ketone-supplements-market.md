<p><strong>The Exogenous Ketone Supplements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ketone Salts</li><li>Ketone Esters</li><li>MCT Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exogenous ketone supplements market comprises three main types: ketone salts, ketone esters, and MCT oil. Ketone salts combine ketones with minerals like sodium or potassium, providing a quick energy source and elevating blood ketone levels. Ketone esters, more complex and potent, are used for rapid ketosis but may taste unpleasant. MCT oil, derived from coconut or palm oil, promotes ketosis by increasing ketone production in the liver. Together, these supplements support energy, weight management, and cognitive function.</p></p>

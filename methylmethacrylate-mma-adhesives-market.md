<p><p>The Methylmethacrylate (MMA) adhesives market is categorized into thermosetting and thermoplastic adhesives. Thermosetting adhesives cure and form a permanent bond when exposed to heat, providing exceptional strength and durability, making them ideal for high-stress applications. In contrast, thermoplastic adhesives remain pliable upon heating and can be reprocessed, offering flexibility and ease of use for quick repairs and bonding dissimilar materials. This diversity in adhesive types caters to various industrial applications, enhancing versatility in bonding solutions.</p></p>

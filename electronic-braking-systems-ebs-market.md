<p><p>Electronic Braking Systems (EBS) enhance vehicle safety and performance by using electronic controls to optimize braking efficiency. The market is primarily divided into two types: Disc EBS and Drum EBS. Disc EBS utilizes disc brakes, offering better heat dissipation and shorter stopping distances, commonly used in passenger vehicles. In contrast, Drum EBS employs drum brakes, often found in heavier vehicles like trucks and buses, providing durability and cost-effectiveness. Both systems aim to improve vehicle stability, control, and overall braking reliability.</p></p>

<p><p>Dead weight testers are precision instruments used for calibrating pressure measurement devices. The market is segmented into three main types: hydraulic, pneumatic, and others. Hydraulic dead weight testers utilize liquids for pressure generation and are ideal for high-pressure scenarios, offering high accuracy and stability. Pneumatic dead weight testers use gases, suitable for lower pressure ranges and offering portability. The "others" category encompasses specialized devices catering to niche applications, combining aspects of both hydraulic and pneumatic technologies for diverse calibration needs.</p></p>

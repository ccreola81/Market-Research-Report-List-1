<p><strong>The Cotton Picker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spindle Picker</li><li>Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cotton picker market consists primarily of two types: spindle pickers and strippers. Spindle pickers utilize rotating spindles to selectively harvest cotton bolls, ensuring minimal damage to the plants and higher quality cotton. In contrast, strippers remove entire bolls from the plant, making them more suitable for large-scale harvesting on expansive fields. While spindle pickers are often preferred for their precision, strippers are valued for their efficiency and speed, catering to different farming needs and operational scales in cotton production.</p></p>

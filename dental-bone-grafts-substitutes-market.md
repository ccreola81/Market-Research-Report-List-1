<p><p>The dental bone grafts and substitutes market encompasses three primary types: allografts, xenografts, and synthetic materials. Allografts utilize bone sourced from human donors, promoting natural integration and healing. Xenografts involve materials derived from animal sources, typically bovine, offering a biocompatible scaffold for tissue regeneration. Synthetic grafts are created from biocompatible materials, mimicking natural bone properties while allowing for customizable applications. Together, these options support dental procedures such as implants and reconstructive surgeries, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>

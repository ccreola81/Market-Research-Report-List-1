<p><p>Bulk bag liners are specialized packaging solutions designed to protect contents during storage and transport. The market comprises various types: LLPE (Low Linear Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility; LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) provides strength and puncture resistance; ULDPE (Ultra-Low-Density Polyethylene) is lightweight yet durable. Combinations like PE/Nylon/EVOH/PE enhance barrier properties for gases and moisture, while PE/Foil/PE adds an effective moisture barrier. Lastly, PE/Metallocene/PE combines advanced polyethylene technology for superior strength and performance, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>

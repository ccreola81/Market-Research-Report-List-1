<p><strong>The Meat Fat Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Meat Fat Analyzer market is primarily segmented into two key types: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) technology and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy) technology. DEXA technology utilizes dual-energy X-ray beams to measure fat composition, providing precise analysis of meat quality. In contrast, NIR technology employs light absorption patterns to assess fat content rapidly and non-destructively. Both methods play critical roles in ensuring meat quality, optimizing production processes, and enhancing food safety in the meat industry.</p></p>

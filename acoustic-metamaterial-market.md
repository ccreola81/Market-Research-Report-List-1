<p><p>The Acoustic Metamaterial market includes several types, such as Resonant Membrane, Periodic Resonators, and Sonic Crystals. Resonant Membranes utilize thin flexible materials to absorb sound through resonant frequencies, making them effective in noise reduction. Periodic Resonators, designed with specific geometries, manipulate sound waves through interference, enhancing sound control. Sonic Crystals consist of periodic structures that create band gaps to block certain sound frequencies, leading to unique sound manipulation properties. Together, these categories enhance acoustic performance in various applications.</p></p>

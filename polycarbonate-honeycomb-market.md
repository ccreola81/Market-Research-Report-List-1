<p><p>The Polycarbonate Honeycomb market is divided into two main types: Monolayer and Multilayer Polycarbonate Honeycomb. Monolayer Polycarbonate Honeycomb consists of a single layer, offering lightweight and basic structural support, making it suitable for applications like signage and lightweight partitions. In contrast, Multilayer Polycarbonate Honeycomb features multiple layers, enhancing strength, thermal insulation, and sound absorption, ideal for use in building facades, roofing, and automotive components. Both types cater to various industrial needs, emphasizing their unique properties and applications.</p></p>

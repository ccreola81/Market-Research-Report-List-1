<p><p>The GI stents market encompasses various types designed for different gastrointestinal applications. Esophageal stents facilitate swallowing in patients with esophageal obstructions, while duodenal stents aid in bypassing blockages in the duodenum. Biliary stents alleviate bile duct obstructions, improving bile flow, and colonic stents are used to treat colorectal blockages. Pancreatic stents help manage complications related to pancreatic disorders by keeping the ducts open. Each stent type addresses specific conditions, enhancing patient care and treatment outcomes in gastrointestinal health.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>DC Type</li><li>AC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber-optic current sensor market is primarily divided into two types: DC Type and AC Type. DC Type sensors are designed to measure direct current, utilizing the magneto-optic effect in optical fibers to detect changes in magnetic fields. AC Type sensors, on the other hand, are tailored for alternating current, often relying on the modulation of light signals in response to varying magnetic fields. Both types are critical for applications in power grids, renewable energy systems, and industrial monitoring.</p></p>

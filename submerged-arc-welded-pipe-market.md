<p><strong>The Submerged Arc Welded Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LSAW Pipe</li><li>SSAW Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submerged arc welded pipe market primarily consists of two types: LSAW (Longitudinally Submerged Arc Welded) and SSAW (Spirally Submerged Arc Welded) pipes. LSAW pipes are produced by rolling flat steel plates into cylindrical shapes and welding along the seam, offering high strength and pressure resistance, making them suitable for large-diameter applications. Conversely, SSAW pipes are created by spirally welding steel strips, allowing for greater flexibility in pipe dimensions and applications in various sectors, including oil and gas, construction, and infrastructure.</p></p>

<p><p>Lead-acid batteries for motorcycles primarily come in two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead Acid) and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries, sealed and maintenance-free, use absorbed glass mat or gel technology to prevent spillage and are suited for various riding conditions. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance and electrolyte level checks, offering higher power output and longer cycling life. Both types cater to diverse motorcycle needs, balancing weight, longevity, and performance.</p></p>

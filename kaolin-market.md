<p><p>Kaolin, also known as china clay, is a naturally occurring white clay mineral composed primarily of kaolinite. It is widely used in various industries, including paper, ceramics, paints, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, due to its properties such as high brightness, fine particle size, and chemical inertness. In the paper industry, kaolin is used as a coating and filler to enhance printability and opacity, while in ceramics, it improves strength and whiteness.</p><p>The Kaolin Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period. Geographically, Asia-Pacific is emerging as a key growth region, driven by rapid industrialization and urbanization. Overall, the kaolin market is poised for steady growth, supported by its versatile applications and expanding industrial demand.</p></p>

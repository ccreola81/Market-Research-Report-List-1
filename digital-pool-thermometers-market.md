<p><p>Digital pool thermometers are essential for monitoring water temperature in pools, with two primary types being the cable probe and wireless floating variants. The cable probe type features a sensor attached to a long cable, allowing accurate readings at various depths while remaining stationary. In contrast, the wireless floating type is a buoyant device that measures temperature and transmits data wirelessly to a display unit, offering convenience and mobility. Both types cater to different user preferences and pool maintenance needs.</p></p>

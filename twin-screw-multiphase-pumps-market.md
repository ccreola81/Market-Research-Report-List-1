<p><strong>The Twin Screw Multiphase Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-screw Pump (TSP)</li><li>Helicoaxial Pump (HAP)</li><li>Progressing-cavity Pump (PCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twin screw multiphase pumps are key in transporting a mixture of fluids, such as oil, gas, and water. The Twin Screw Pump (TSP) utilizes two intermeshing screws for efficient flow, ideal for high-viscosity fluids. Helicoaxial Pumps (HAP) employ helical rotors for handling multiphase flows with minimal turbulence, enhancing efficiency. Progressing-Cavity Pumps (PCP) feature a single rotor within a cavity, providing steady flow and handling variable viscosities effectively. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, improving fluid handling in various sectors.</p></p>

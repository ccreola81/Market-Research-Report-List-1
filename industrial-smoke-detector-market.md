<p><p>The industrial smoke detector market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Photoelectric Smoke Alarms** use a light source and sensor to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires.</p><p>2. **Ionization Smoke Alarms** utilize radioactive material to sense smoke, responding quickly to flaming fires, though they may be less effective for smoldering types.</p><p>3. **Dual Sensor Smoke Alarms** combine both technologies, providing comprehensive detection capabilities for varying fire scenarios, enhancing safety in industrial environments.</p></p>

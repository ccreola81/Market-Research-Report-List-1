<p><p>The Radiographic Breast Localization Device market is primarily categorized into three types: Wire Localization, Localization Coil, and Others. </p><p>Wire Localization involves inserting a thin wire into the breast to guide surgeons during procedures, commonly used for breast biopsies. Localization Coil utilizes a small coil placed in the breast to enhance visibility under imaging techniques, aiding in precise tissue removal. The "Others" category encompasses alternative localization methods, such as radioactive seed localization or ultrasound-guided approaches, broadening the scope of breast cancer treatment options.</p></p>

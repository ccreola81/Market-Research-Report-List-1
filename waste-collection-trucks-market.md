<p><p>Waste collection trucks can be categorized into several types based on their loading mechanisms and specific uses. Front loaders feature a hydraulic arm for lifting and emptying containers, while rear loaders have a hopper at the back for manual loading of waste. Side loaders utilize a robotic arm for automated collection from the side. Pneumatic collection systems employ vacuum methods for efficient waste gathering. Grapple trucks are equipped with mechanical arms for bulky items, and liquid tankers are designed for transporting liquid waste.</p></p>

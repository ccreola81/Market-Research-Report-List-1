<p><ul><li>SWIR Area Scan Camera</li><li>SWIR Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SWIR camera market encompasses two primary types: SWIR area scan cameras and SWIR line scan cameras. SWIR area scan cameras capture a full image in a single exposure, making them ideal for applications requiring quick analysis, such as quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, SWIR line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, generally suited for continuous processes like web inspections in industries such as food, textiles, and printing, where high-speed performance is essential.</p></p>

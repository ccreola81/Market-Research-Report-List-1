<p><strong>The Microwave Infrared Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave infrared detectors market is divided into active and passive types. Active detectors generate their own signals to detect infrared radiation, enhancing sensitivity and precision, often used in advanced surveillance and imaging systems. In contrast, passive detectors rely on external infrared radiation emitted by objects, making them suitable for energy-efficient applications such as thermal imaging and motion detection. Both types serve various industries, including security, automotive, and industrial applications, catering to diverse detection needs.</p></p>

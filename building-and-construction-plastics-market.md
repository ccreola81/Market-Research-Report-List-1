<p><ul><li>Thermoplastics</li><li>Thermosetting Plastics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Building and Construction Plastics market consists primarily of thermoplastics and thermosetting plastics. Thermoplastics, such as polyethylene and polyvinyl chloride, are characterized by their ability to soften and reshape when heated, making them versatile for various applications like pipes and insulation. In contrast, thermosetting plastics, including epoxy and phenolic resins, undergo a chemical change when cured, leading to a rigid, heat-resistant final product ideal for structural components and adhesives. Together, these plastics play crucial roles in modern construction and design.</p></p>

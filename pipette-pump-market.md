<p><p>The pipette pump market is primarily divided into two types: manual pipette pumps and electrical pipette pumps. Manual pipette pumps operate through a simple hand-press mechanism, allowing users to control liquid aspiration and dispensing with precision and minimal training. In contrast, electrical pipette pumps utilize powered mechanisms to automate the liquid handling process, offering faster and more consistent results, often preferred in high-throughput laboratories. Each type caters to specific user needs based on efficiency, ease of use, and application.</p></p>

<p><strong>The PCB Photo Plotters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Drum Laser Photoplotters</li><li>Internal Drum Laser Photoplotters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB photo plotters market is divided into two main types: external drum laser photoplotters and internal drum laser photoplotters. External drum photoplotters utilize a rotating drum outside the laser assembly, allowing for larger substrates and faster processing speeds. In contrast, internal drum photoplotters have the drum integrated within the machine, offering precise registration and better control over layer density. Both technologies serve the crucial function of producing high-resolution images for printed circuit board manufacturing, impacting efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>

<p><p>The flame retardant tape market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided adhesive tapes. Single-sided adhesive tape features a sticky side that adheres to surfaces, offering protection against flames while maintaining easy application. In contrast, double-sided adhesive tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing it to bond two surfaces together while providing flame resistance. Both types are vital in enhancing safety across various industries by preventing fire spread and meeting regulatory standards, making them essential in construction and manufacturing applications.</p></p>

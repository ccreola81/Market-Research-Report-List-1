<p><ul><li>Gloss Method</li><li>Gravimetric Method</li><li>Haze Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fogging tester market encompasses three primary types: the Gloss Method, Gravimetric Method, and Haze Method. The Gloss Method assesses the gloss retention of materials exposed to fogging conditions, indicating clarity and aesthetic quality. The Gravimetric Method measures the weight of condensed fogging deposits, providing insight into material performance and contamination levels. The Haze Method evaluates the optical clarity by determining the light scattering in samples, crucial for applications where transparency is vital, ensuring quality standards in various industries.</p></p>

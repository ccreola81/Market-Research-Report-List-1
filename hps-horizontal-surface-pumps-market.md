<p><ul><li>Conventional</li><li>Electric Side Mount</li><li>Gas Driven Side Mount</li><li>The Mini Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal Surface Pumps (HPS) are categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. Conventional HPS are traditional pumps typically used for general water transfer applications. Electric Side Mount pumps are powered by electricity and designed for ease of installation and operation, ideal for scenarios with access to electrical power. Gas Driven Side Mount pumps offer mobility and independence from electrical sources, making them suitable for remote locations. The Mini Unit market focuses on compact, portable pumps for smaller applications, providing convenient solutions where space is limited.</p></p>

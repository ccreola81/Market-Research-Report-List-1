<p><p>Document control software is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control over data security and compliance, but requires significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. This type typically offers lower initial costs and easier collaboration across remote teams, making it popular among businesses.</p></p>

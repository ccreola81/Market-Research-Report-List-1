<p><p>The nondestructive testing (NDT) equipment market encompasses various methods used to evaluate the properties of materials without causing damage. Ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws. Radiography testing uses X-rays or gamma rays for internal inspection. Visual inspection involves direct observation for visible defects. Magnetic particle testing detects surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Electromagnetic testing and eddy-current testing identify flaws based on electromagnetic induction. Liquid penetrant testing reveals surface cracks, while thermography, acoustic emission, terahertz imaging, and infrared testing fall under other advanced techniques for detecting anomalies without material alteration.</p></p>

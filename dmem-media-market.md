High glucose DMEM is typically used for rapidly proliferating cells, such as most cancer cell lines, providing necessary energy for growth. In contrast, low glucose DMEM is suited for more differentiated or slower-growing cell types, offering a reduced energy supply beneficial for metabolic studies and certain tissue culture applications.</p></p>

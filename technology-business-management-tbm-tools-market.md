<p><p>Technology Business Management (TBM) tools are categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise TBM tools are installed and run locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT resources. Cloud-Based TBM tools, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing scalability, ease of implementation, and reduced upfront costs while streamlining maintenance and updates through the service provider.</p></p>

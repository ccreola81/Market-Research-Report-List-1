<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is a high-performance polymer known for its exceptional chemical resistance and low friction properties. The PTFE market is categorized into two main types: particles PTFE and fine powder PTFE. Particles PTFE is typically utilized in various industrial applications for coatings and gaskets due to its robustness. Fine powder PTFE, on the other hand, is primarily employed in applications requiring a more precise texture, such as in lubricants and advanced manufacturing processes, offering improved performance and compatibility with diverse materials.</p></p>

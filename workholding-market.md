<p><ul><li>Milling/drilling workholding</li><li>Turning workholding</li><li>Grinding workholding</li><li>EDM workholding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The workholding market encompasses specialized solutions for various machining processes. Milling/drilling workholding includes vises, clamps, and fixtures designed to secure workpieces during cutting operations. Turning workholding focuses on devices like chucks and collets that hold cylindrical materials for lathe operations. Grinding workholding systems, including magnetic and vacuum devices, stabilize parts for surface finishing. EDM workholding incorporates tooling that ensures precision and stability during electrical discharge machining processes, all essential for optimizing productivity and accuracy in manufacturing.</p></p>

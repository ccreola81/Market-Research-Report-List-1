<p><p>Water modeling software can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a user's hardware, providing full control over data and custom configurations but requiring significant maintenance resources. Conversely, cloud-based software runs on remote servers and is accessed via the internet, offering scalability, easier collaboration, and reduced upfront costs. Each type serves different organizational needs, with on-premise solutions often favored for large, established entities, while cloud-based models appeal to more flexible, cost-conscious users.</p></p>
